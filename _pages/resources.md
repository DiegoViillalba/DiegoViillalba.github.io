## 🌍 Research Internships & International Opportunities in Physics

This page collects resources mentioned in my seminar  
**“Cosmología sin fronteras: ¿Cómo conseguir una estancia internacional de investigación y no morir en el intento?”**  
(SEF – Facultad de Ciencias, UNAM).

The goal is to provide **practical starting points** for undergraduate physics students interested in international research experiences.

---

## 🔬 Physics Research Internships & Programs

### 🔹 General Internship Databases
These platforms aggregate research opportunities across institutions and subfields.

- **Pathways to Science (Physics)**  
  https://www.pathwaystoscience.org/Discipline.aspx?sort=ENG-Physics_Physics  
  A comprehensive database of funded programs, especially useful for undergraduates.

- **Physics Today Jobs – Internships**  
  https://jobs.physicstoday.org/jobs/intern/  
  Includes research internships, summer programs, and industry placements.

- **Gradcracker – Physics Internships (UK & Europe)**  
  https://www.gradcracker.com/search/science/physics-work-placements-internships  
  Useful for internships and placements in Europe.

---

### 🔹 University & Institute-Specific Programs

- **MIT – Summer Research Opportunities**  
  https://sps.mit.edu/summer-research-opportunities/  
  Centralized page for MIT summer research programs.

- **University of Oxford – Physics Summer Internships**  
  https://www.physics.ox.ac.uk/research/summer-internships-undergraduates  
  Undergraduate-focused research internships in physics.

- **Stanford University – Research Opportunities Outside Stanford**  
  https://physics.stanford.edu/undergraduate/undergraduate-research/research-opportunities-outside-stanford  
  Excellent curated list of external research opportunities.

- **Project YL (Europe-focused internships)**  
  https://projectyl.github.io/inter/  
  A growing collection of international research programs.

---

### 🔹 Programs for Mexican Students

- **AMEXCID – International Opportunities for Mexicans**  
  https://www.gob.mx/amexcid/acciones-y-programas/oferta-para-mexicanos  
  Scholarships and exchange programs supported by the Mexican government.

- **Mitacs (Canada)**  
  https://www.mitacs.ca/  
  Highly recommended for research stays in Canada (Globalink, Accelerate).

## 🧠 Preparing Your Academic Profile

Research groups do not only evaluate grades.  
They look for **clarity of interests, initiative, and evidence of work**.

---

### 📄 Academic CV

- **CV Template (Harshibars – Overleaf)**  
  https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates/harshibars-resume/sbcyynmtpnyd  
  Clean, academic-friendly format.

- **How to Write an Academic CV**  
  https://grad.illinois.edu/professional-development/applying-jobs/cv-or-resume  
  Clear explanation of the differences between CVs and resumes.

---

### 🌐 Academic Website

- **AcademicPages (GitHub Template)**  
  https://github.com/academicpages/academicpages.github.io  
  Widely used by researchers and students.

- **Bootstrap Personal Academic Templates**  
  https://bootstrapmade.com/bootstrap-personal-templates/  
  Alternative designs if you prefer a custom layout.

---

### 💻 Bonus Resources (Highly Recommended)

These are not mandatory, but **add significant value** to your profile:

- GitHub repositories with documented projects
- Posters or slides from seminars
- Technical notes or mini-reviews
- Small independent research projects

> *You don’t need to look like a senior researcher —  
> you need to look like a serious researcher in training.*

---

## 🎯 Final Advice

International research opportunities are not reserved for a select few.  
They reward **initiative, preparation, and consistency**.

Start early, document your work, and don’t wait until you feel “ready”.
